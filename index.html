FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What time does the event start?

The event starts at 7:00 PM with the opening act, followed by Arijit Singh's performance at 8:30 PM.

How can I get to the venue?

The venue is accessible by public transport and has ample parking space for those driving.

Are there any age restrictions for the event?

The event is open to all ages, but children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.

What should I bring to the event?

Please bring your ticket, a valid ID, and any necessary items for your comfort. Outside food and drinks are not allowed.

Is there a dress code?

There is no strict dress code, but we recommend casual and comfortable attire.

VENUE DETAILS

The event will be held at the Pune International Convention Center, located in the heart of Pune. The venue features state-of-the-art facilities, ample parking, and is easily accessible from various parts of the city.
